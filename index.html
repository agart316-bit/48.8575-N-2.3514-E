Button History

The Element

<button> was introduced in HTML 2.0 in 1995 — but buttons as physical objects predate the internet by centuries. A button on a garment and a button on a screen share the same name, the same purpose: press me, something happens.

25 <button> elements, placed on a globe as pins. Each styled to look like a button from its era — pearl in the 1920s, neon in the 80s, flat in the 2010s — mirroring how interface design evolved alongside fashion.

The Concept

Fashion history is geography. The capital of style never stayed in one place — it migrated from Paris to London to New York to Tokyo to Seoul to everywhere and nowhere at once.

Spin the globe. Find a button. Press it. Something happens.
